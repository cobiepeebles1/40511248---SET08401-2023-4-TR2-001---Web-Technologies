CHOCOLATE MOLTEN CAKE

How to make guide

Steps

  • 1.Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan
  • 2.Put chocolate and butter into bowl of hot water then stir until smooth
  • 3.Mix the sugar, eggs and vanilla extract together and then enter the flour
  • 4.Bake for 10 minutes then cake should be ready

Ingredients

For the cake

  • 100 g butter
  • 100 g light brown sugar
  • 100 g dark chocolate
  • 3 larges eggs
  • half tsp vanilla extract
  • 50 g flour
  • optional - cream
