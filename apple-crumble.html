APPLE CRUMBLE

How to make guide

Steps

  • 1.Heat the oven to 190C/170 fan
  • 2.Use 570g peeled, cored and sliced apples with 2 tbsp golden caster sugar and put in a 20cm square dish.
  • 3.170g of flour and 110g golden caster sugar in a bowl with a generous pinch of salt.
  • 4.Slice in 110g cold butter and rub it in until moist.
  • 5.Pour the crumb mix over the apples to form a pile in the centre, even out after
  • 6.Set on a baking tray and put in the oven for 35-40 minutes, wait until golden
  • 7.Dish is ready to be served after 10/15 minutes enjoy!

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 570g apples
  • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar

For the crumble

  • 175g flour
  • 110g golden caster sugar
  • 110g  butter
