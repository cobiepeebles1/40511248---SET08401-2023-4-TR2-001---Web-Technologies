BLACK FORREST GATEAU

How to make guide

Steps

  • 1.Heat the oven to 160C/140 fan
  • 2.microwave the dark chocolate and butter until it melts then put granules into 120ml boiling water and mix add the coffee to the chocolate/butter mix stir well .
  • 3.In another bowl use th Flour, cocoa Powder, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, golden caster sugar, and brown sugar and mix well again
  • 4.Mix the eggs with the buttermilk then add both bowls
  • 5.put in prefered baking tins then oven for 25-30 minutes
  • 6.Once ready put on desired extras such as cherries or cream

Ingredients

For the cake

  • 225 g Dark Chocolate
  • 1 tsp Instant Coffee Granules
  • 1 tsp Instant Coffee Granules
  • 170 g Flour
  • 22.5 g Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 215 g Golden Caster Sugar
  • 200 g Light Brown Sugar
  • 4 Medium Eggs
  • 1/4 tsp Bicarbonate of Soda
  • 225 g Butter
  • cherries
