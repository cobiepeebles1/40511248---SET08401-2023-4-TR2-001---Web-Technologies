LEMON DRIZZLE CAKE

How to make guide

Steps

  • 1.Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan
  • 2.mix butter and sugar together until creamy, add eggs one at a time slowly
  • 3.mix the flour and lemon until mixed well
  • 4.Line a loaf tin with greaseproof paper, and add mixture
  • 5.bake for 45 minutes
  • 6.When waiting mix the drizzle ingredients
  • 7.When ready make holes with fork then pour drizzle on to it

Ingredients

For the cake

  • 225 g butter
  • 225 g sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 225 g flour
  • 1 lemon

For the drizzle

  • 2 lemons juiced
  • 90 g sugar
